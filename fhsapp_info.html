FHS APP Information

A place where you can find helpful tools and resources regarding the FHS Application and MUCH MORE!

The app acts as a prestigeous resource to view assignments from all participating teachers as well as additonal information regarding

  • Overall school announcements
  • A calender of the school year
  • Personalized "Settings" to the student's unique class schedule

The app was created by a team of intelligent brain power at our very own, Franklin High School. These students, with much assistance from Gage (the advisor), are thoroughly working with the computer coding that goes along with creating a wonderful website- designed to help all students communicate through technology with their teachers and FHS!
